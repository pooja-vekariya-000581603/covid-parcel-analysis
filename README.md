# Final Project: Impact of COVID-19 on ABC Company’s Parcel Delivery Business

## Group Assignment
This repository contains our final project submission analysing the impact of COVID-19 on ABC Company’s parcel delivery business.

## Files Included
- `notebook/Final_Project_COVID_Parcel.ipynb`  
  Python notebook used for data loading, ISGR calculation, COVID timing analysis, peak-season comparison, customer categorisation, proxy-group analysis, and estimated revenue analysis.

- `presentation/COVID Parcel Presentation.pptx`  
  Executive-style presentation summarising the business findings.

- `powerbi/COVID Parcel Dashboard.pbix`  
  Power BI dashboard built from cleaned summary outputs.

- `powerbi/PowerBI_Ready_Summary_Tables.xlsx`  
  Summary workbook used to accelerate Power BI dashboard design.

- `data/COVID_Parcel_Business.csv`  
  Source dataset used in the analysis.

## Key Findings
- The Industry Standard Growth Rate (ISGR) was calculated as 11.40%.
- Parcel volume impact became clearly visible around Week 12 of 2020.
- The 2020 peak season outperformed the 2019 peak season.
- Customer outcomes were mixed, with both strong growth and decline across the portfolio.
- Official customer-group labels and discount tiers were not provided, so proxy groups and gross revenue estimates were used transparently.

## Notes on Assumptions
- Proxy customer groups were created using 2019 annual parcel-volume quartiles.
- Revenue was estimated at the base parcel rate of $22 because contract discount tiers were not provided.
